Nami: The Tidecaller

About Nami

A headstrong young vastaya of the seas, Nami uses her mystical Tidecaller staff to reshape the tides and defend her fellow Marai from danger.
The first of her kind to leave the ocean and venture onto dry land, Nami faces the unthinkable with grit, determination, and daring mettle.

Characteristics

  • Species: Marai
  • Place of origin: Marai Village, Mount Targon
